The Beginning (2024)

Founded in 2024, Getly Project Shop emerged from a shared vision among passionate CSE graduates who understood the real struggles students face. We recognized the gap between academic requirements and available resources, and decided to bridge it.

What started as a peer-to-peer assistance initiative quickly evolved into a comprehensive project development platform. Today, we've helped hundreds of students achieve academic excellence while building their confidence and understanding.
